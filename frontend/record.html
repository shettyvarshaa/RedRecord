Introduction to Cadaver Dissection

Cadaver dissection is a fundamental part of medical education, providing an unparalleled opportunity to study human anatomy in a hands-on manner. It allows students to explore the complex structures of the human body, understand spatial relationships between organs, and gain insights into anatomical variations.

Preparation for Dissection
